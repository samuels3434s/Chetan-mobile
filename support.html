How to Apply

Applying Mobile Skins

Step 1: Wipe the back glass of your phone with wipes. Ensure no grease or lint remains.

Step 2: Peel the back skin layer slightly and align it precisely with the camera lens cutout, flash, and speaker grids.

Step 3: Once aligned, stick the skin slowly using a plastic card or your thumb to slide out bubbles from the center outward.

Step 4: Heat the corners/edges with a hairdryer for 4–5 seconds and press them firmly for a flat, bubble-free adhesive fit.

Applying Laptop Skins

Step 1: Wipe the laptop lid to clear dirt and dust particles.

Step 2: Align the skin starting from the back edge (near the screen hinge) and standard apple/brand logo cutout.

Step 3: Peel the backing paper back gradually while sliding a card to apply pressure across the surface.

Step 4: Cut off any excess margin if you purchased a universal size skin. Run a warm card along the edge to secure binding.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the skins leave sticky residue when removed?

No. We use genuine 3M vinyl wrapping materials. The adhesive is designed to remove clean without leaving any sticky residue behind, even after years of use.

Does it make the phone or laptop thick?

Not at all. Our skins are only 0.2mm thick. They protect against scratches and scuffs while keeping the sleek profile of your device completely intact.

Can I apply a cover over a phone skin?

Yes, our skins add almost zero thickness, so you can easily put clear shockproof TPU covers right over the mobile skin.

How does a card skin work at ATMs?

Our card skins are thin enough that they do not get stuck in card slots. Choosing the chip cutout option ensures your chip is fully exposed for tap and dip transactions.
